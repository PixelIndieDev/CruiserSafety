# Cruiser Safety
A simple mod that removes the damage players can receive from the company cruiser crashing, while preserving the camera shake effects.
Now, your teammates can truly become the danger.

## Installation
1. Install `BepInEx`
2. Drop 'BepInEx' **from the CruiserSafety.zip** into your Lethal Company folder.

## What it does
- Drivers and passengers no longer take damage or die from crashes

## What it doesn't do
- Does not affect damage dealt to enemies by the Cruiser
- Does not affect the Cruiser's own HP or destruction
- Does not affect any other sources of damage

## Usage
No configuration needed.

> All players in the lobby are required to have the `Cruiser Safety` mod installed. Clients without the mod will receive an generic "an error occurred" message and be sent back to the main menu.

## Compatibility
This mod only modifies the DamagePlayerInVehicle behavior and should work alongside most other mods.
